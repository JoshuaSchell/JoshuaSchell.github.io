The best way to learn is to share what you're learning.

## Why It Works

When you write about something, you have to understand it deeply. You can't fake it.

> "If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough."

## How to Start

1. **Pick a topic** you're learning
2. **Write a blog post** explaining it
3. **Share it** on Twitter/X
4. **Repeat**

## Don't Worry About Being Wrong

Everyone starts somewhere. The experts you follow today were once beginners too.

```javascript
const journey = {
  start: "knowing nothing",
  process: "learning in public",
  result: "expertise over time"
};
```

## Benefits

- Build an audience
- Solidify your knowledge
- Help others
- Create opportunities
